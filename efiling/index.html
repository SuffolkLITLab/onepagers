Flexible, user-friendly e-filing tools

The Suffolk LIT Lab is a non-profit e-filing service provider (EFSP), with e-filing tools built to simplify e-filing, especially for self-represented litigants. Our EFSP can accept e-filing "bundles" from two sources:

Docassemble

Online guided interviews with integrated document assembly and e-filing are the gold standard for user experience and access to justice. With our integration, Docassemble can e-file a user's completed court forms without leaving the interview.

LITEfile

Outside of guided interviews, LITEfile offers users a simplified e-filing user interface. It reduces complexity for the user while complying with technical e-filing requirements.
