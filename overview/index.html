Open-source tools and affordable services for court forms, guided interviews, and e-filing, from the Suffolk Legal Innovation and Technology Lab (LIT Lab).

Free software and support

Free and open-source software for building and e-filing court forms, supported by an active community of courts, legal aid organizations, and volunteers.

AssemblyLine tools

Docassemble software extensions for building guided interviews, including the Weaver, a library of translated questions, and other tools.

E-filing software

E-filing proxy server, integrations for court e-filing systems, and LITEfile, a general-purpose e-filing tool for self-represented litigants.

Community support

Meetings, workshops, an online support forum, training, documentation, and other resources.

Learn more and get started on the Document Assembly Line website.

Affordable Docassemble hosting and EFSP

Managed Docassemble hosting for courts and legal aid organizations, and a nonprofit e-filing service provider (EFSP) for courts that integrates with Docassemble interviews and LITEfile.

Docassemble hosting

Affordable hosting with the AssemblyLine tools, configured for the needs (and budgets) of courts and legal aid organizations.

EFSP with LITEfile

Nonprofit e-filing service provider (EFSP) with LITEfile for court e-filing systems.
