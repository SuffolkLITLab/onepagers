Free & Open-Source Components

The following core components are free and open source thanks to an active community of courts, legal aid organizations, and volunteers.

CourtFormsOnline

A user-friendly, mobile-first, customizable online interview portal website for Docassemble servers.

Assembly Line Software

Extensions for Docassemble, including a library of pre-built and pre-translated questions, the Weaver, form stats, automated testing, and more.

E-Filing

An open-source e-filing service provider (EFSP) that integrates Docassemble interviews with court e-filing systems, including Tyler's eFile & Serve.

Community Support

Meetings, workshops, and an online forum for sharing knowledge and troubleshooting problems, plus training, documentation, and other resources.
